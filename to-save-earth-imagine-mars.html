To Save Earth, Imagine Mars

Why we shouldn’t colonize Mars, but invest the resources in a livable Earth instead (and why a younger generation’s ‘Martian Constitution’ matters)

Auteur: R.G.C. Hoek

Bekijk de omslag in vol formaat
